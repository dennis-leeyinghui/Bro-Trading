我們的故事 About Us

我們很小，但在成長。　We are small, but growing.

「巴打」兩字源自粵語音譯Brother，一般可用作稱呼熟識的朋友。
成立於2017年， 我們以「特色產品，大眾價格」為宗旨，希望從「巴打」的角度出發，為從世界各地挑選最能滿足顧客口味的產品，以無私照料的心態為廣大顧客服務。

"Bro Trading" comes from the word "brother", a word that could be used to call your close friends. Founded in 2017 with the motto "Unique Products. General Prices", we strive to source the best and most unique food products from across the globe to touch our customers' taste buds and hearts from the perspective of a "brother". We work hard to take care of our customers with selfless devotion.
